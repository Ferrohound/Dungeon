Dungeon Crawler to test procedural generation, GA programming & 
Dialogue System.

---------------------

Tower of power.
3 stages of power, reach the top for your finest hour.
The player K. Iku claws their way to power to destroy a mysterious amorphous entity
dwelling in the underbelly of their small island nation.

White noise, betrayal, destruction.
Can you conquer fate and prevent the untimely end of your nation?
